Our Story

A Legacy of Warmth
Since 1977

Kali Dham Guest House, named after Late Shri Kalidas Dutta, has been welcoming guests to the holy city of Puri since 1977. Founded by Late Shri Tarak Nath Dutta and carried forward through three generations of the Dutta family, today managed by Rajib Dutta.

With 37 well-maintained rooms — including AC rooms, sea-view rooms, and rooms with attached kitchens — we offer the comfort of home, just 3 minutes from the Bay of Bengal.
